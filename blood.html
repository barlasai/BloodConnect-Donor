Donation Eligibility Criteria

Please review these guidelines to ensure a safe and successful donation experience for everyone.

Age Requirement

You must be between 18 and 65 years old.

Weight Requirement

You must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).

Good Health

You must be in good general health and feel well.

No Recent Procedures

No tattoos or piercings in the last 6 months.
